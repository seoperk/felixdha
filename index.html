Know the Regulators

DHA — Dubai Health Authority

Governs most of Dubai outside DHCC. Uses the Sheryan portal. Requires DataFlow (PSV), exams/assessments, and facility linkage.

MOHAP — Ministry of Health & Prevention

Oversees licensing across several emirates. Processes are similar but fee schedules and criteria may differ.

DOH — Department of Health Abu Dhabi

Regulates Abu Dhabi. Prior DOH experience can help profile recognition but local steps still apply.

FAQ

Do I need a consultancy to get licensed?

No, you can apply directly. Many clinicians use consultants to avoid re‑submissions and stay on top of verification/exam logistics.

How long does a typical DHA licensing journey take?

Often 6–12 weeks depending on documents, PSV response times, exam slots and facility linkage.

Will a consultant speed things up?

They can reduce avoidable delays (missing fields, date mismatches, unclear roles) and coordinate follow‑ups, which often shortens the calendar.
